Making an RL Agent Trade Power Like a Real Market Player

MDP modeling, POMDP reality, and an RL agent trying to make money in between.

What I'm Actually Building

A reinforcement learning agent that participates in ISO day-ahead electricity markets. It looks at historical load, price signals, and forecasts, learns how to place bids, and tries to make economically sensible decisions instead of just doing math tricks in a vacuum.

We model the market as an MDP for tractability, even though real electricity markets behave much closer to a POMDP — partial observability, noisy forecasts, and strategic participants included. The agent builds its state from demand and price signals, selects hourly bidding strategies, and updates its policy based on realized profit.
